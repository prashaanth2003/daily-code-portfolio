📖 How to Play & Game Manual

  • 🟩 Collect Green Commit Blocks to keep your streak going. Each green block adds +1 day to your streak!
  • ❄️ Collect Streak Freezes (glowing shields) to save you if you collide with a system error!
  • ❌ Avoid Red Bugs (Server Down, Merge Conflicts, NullPointer, Legacy Code) which instantly break your streak!
  • 🛡️ Streak Freeze Protection: If you hit an obstacle with a freeze in stock, it's auto-consumed to shield you!
  • 🎹 Keyboard Shortcuts: Spacebar to jump, Shift/F to use shield, R to restart game quickly.
  • 🎵 Web Audio Synthesizer: Plays retro-inspired 8-bit arcade noise entirely generated by code!
